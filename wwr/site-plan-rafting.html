Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site will be to provide information for white water rafting so that those intersted will be able to plan trips accordingly.

Audience

The target audience is adventure seeking individuals or group of individuals that love the outdoors and nature. Families of the same interest are also our target. Q. Who are the target customers? A. Young middle aged (20-30 year old) singles. Also families. Q. What do they want? A. Learn about location, prices, rivers, equipment. Q. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Pictures of the site, location, prices.
